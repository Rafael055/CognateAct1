History

The group was originally named "OGAE,” inspired by our cognate project titled “Optimizing Growth: Arduino-Enabled Greenhouse with Auto Watering System Manual.” Over time, the name evolved into “OGAY,” since our group grew bigger.

Why the name "OGAY"?

The name “OGAY” was chosen as a simpler, catchier version of the original "OGAE.” It symbolizes teamwork, and the fun yet meaningful journey of turning concepts into real prototypes.

What is OGAY?

OGAY is a group dedicated to developing prototypes that transform ideas into practical solutions. We aim to address challenges, spark innovation, and inspire others to explore new possibilities through creativity and collaboration.
